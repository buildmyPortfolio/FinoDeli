Our Story

Where Every Bite
Tells a Story

"The First Wine Bar / Delicatessen in Marikina."

Fino Deli brings rustic Italian and Spanish flare — Tapas, Pizza, Pasta and more — in an inviting and relaxed atmosphere. We serve a wide variety of imported wines, cheeses, and cold cuts.

Fino Deli is about attention to quality and a satisfying dining experience. Our goal is to give our community a chance to enjoy something truly unique.
